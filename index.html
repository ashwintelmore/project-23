It Is In Giving That You Recieve

People often say that we increase the love we have by giving it away. In here, we say, generosity is LOVE.

Giving with Love or call it Charity, is about reaching out to others and not expecting anything in return. It is a pure act of kindness. The More meaningful about giving, is how it is drawing our hearts toward God, storing our treasures in heaven, and empowering us to serve him over serving money to His children in need. According to the United Nations, over half the world population, including 1 billion children, lives in poverty. Charitable organizations help provide millions of people with a path out of poverty into a healthier, brighter future.

Giving also makes you look good other than makes you feel good. Charity is a beautiful expression of compassion and generosity. Generosity has been found to reduce stress, increase longevity, and produce happiness hormones like dopamine in the brain. Think how awesome you would feel when you help alleviate suffering, educate the deserving youths, feed the poor and create positive changes in their lives? You will receive something too when you give, an immeasurable return.

There was a study that "the more generous Americans are, the more happiness, health, and purpose in life they enjoy. Thus in giving you will receive. We aim to reach out those have not served yet. Hoping that we too can change lives.

To Give A Future

Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.

To give them a future is to educate them. Education will give them a better tomorrow in their chosen field of study. To Give An Opportunity

Failure is simply the opportunity to begin again... - Henry Ford

To give an opportunity is to give those who have fallen to 'bad luck', a second chance in life. This could be due to bad decisions in the past, divorces, homelessness, or being hooked to certain kinds of addictions before. They now have overcome and recovered from those situations. And looking for new opportunities.

To give an opportunity is reaching out to people with a path out of poverty into a healthier, brighter new life.

In another light, a person has found a new idea but has no means to make it happen. They have now past their youths and possibly living alone. It is like being stuck in a pit. A new opportunity is like restarting their lives.

Keep in mind that to help someone is an opportunity not only for the recipient but also for the giver.

To Give To The Needy

Giving. It Makes You Feel Good. Generosity Makes You Happy.

To give to the needy is mostly to give food or financial aid so they can get food. This helps counter poverty, and hunger at the same improve their health. If you cannot feed a hundred people, then feed just one. Monetary donations give them the flexibility to purchase a variety of foods, including perishable and non-perishable food. Aid provides those living in extreme poverty with the essential resources necessary to attain a better standard of living.

Our organization does not send food to the needy, we send cash instead, directly to the one posting. Those who are posting their needs, are asking you to help them feed themselves and/or for their family.

There are other reasons to give to the needy as there are many other situations in which donating to them will answer these needs. However, we do not validate these needs or ask for proofs, please read our disclaimer statement. We do our best to remove those we suspect are not realistic.

To Give To The Sick

It is not how much we give but how much love we put into giving. -- Mother Theresa

To give to the sick is easy to understand. We all have been ill at one time or another. Imagine how difficult it is to be sick and have no money to pay for the medical bills and even for the medications. Catastrophic illness can happen at any time and to anybody. Out-of-pocket costs associated with an illness or rare disease can burden patients and families financially. This is when families ask for your financial help. They do their best to raise money or seek financial help. Fundraising for medical bills can truly make a positive impact on the lives of those who need it. But not all can do any kind of fundraising activities and no one will do it for them.

We are talking about the people posting here: with no medical assistance from their government, and no health insurance. They have no other option but to ask people like you who will listen and give them some financial help for their urgent medical needs.

This is the time to step in. Consider it, an opportunity to help. Have a heart.
